eSports Team

Many of our members have top finishes in the highest levels of competition i.e. Yu-Gi- Oh! Championship Series and ARG Circuit Series. Gaming Arena

About Shenanigans Gaming and Esports Arena: We are Shenanigans Gaming. We are in the entertainment industry providing video and table-top game players a venue to play and compete with their friends, other local community members and players from around the world. We plan to host daily video game and trading card game competitions, watch parties for major tournaments and late night gaming events. We will also host computer literacy camps for children and vend trading cards in store and online.
